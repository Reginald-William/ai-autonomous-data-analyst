Business Name: TechMart Electronics
Business Type: Electronics Retail Chain
Operating Since: 2020

Business Overview:
TechMart is an electronics retail chain operating across 4 regions in India.
The business sells consumer electronics including Laptops, Phones and Tablets.
Each store operates independently but reports to a central analytics system.

Regions:
- North: Delhi NCR stores
- South: Chennai and surrounding areas
- East: Kolkata metropolitan area
- West: Mumbai and Pune stores

Revenue:
- All revenue figures are in Indian Rupees (INR)
- Revenue represents gross revenue before tax
- Refunds and returns are not deducted from revenue figures
- Each row in the dataset represents a single transaction

Products:
- Laptop: High value item, average transaction value 12000-18000 INR
- Phone: Mid range item, average transaction value 7500-10000 INR
- Tablet: Mid range item, average transaction value 9000-15000 INR

Business Goals:
- Track monthly revenue trends across regions
- Identify best performing regions and products
- Monitor seasonal patterns in sales
- Compare product performance across regions
